What we do

MALUHIA HOSPICE IS DEDICATED TO SERVING OUR COMMUNITY, OUR PATIENTS, AND OUR FAMILIES WITH A TOTAL COMMITMENT TO EXCELLENCE IN HOSPICE CARE. OUR PATIENTS AND FAMILIES COME TO US WITH UNIQUE FEELINGS AND APPREHENSION AS THEY FACE END-OF-LIFE CONCERNS. IT IS OUR COMMITMENT TO OFFER HOPE, ENRICH, AND PROVIDE CUSTOMIZED SUPPORT TO EACH OF OUR PATIENTS AND THEIR FAMILIES. THANK YOU FOR VISITING WITH US.

Hospice Care

Informative

Educate and involve patient and caregivers regarding end-of-life care services.

Provisions

Provisions of holistic treatment(physical, social, emotional, and spiritual) for the patient and family.

Team Care

Effective use of hospice team: Physician nurses, social workers, and therapist working together to provide high-quality care.

Better Future

Bereavement support to follow up for the family.

Mission

Maluhia Hospice Care, PLLC is committed to our mission to treat all patients and families with peace, tranquility and serenity during the end of life with highest level of qualtity care, respect, compassion and support.
