Get to Know Us

Maluhia Hospice Care, PLLC is a nurse-owned and operated hospice agency dedicated to providing end of life care for the terminally ill. “Maluhia” is a Hawaiian word that means “peace, quiet, security, tranquility and solemn.” As Maluhia is a practice of peace and tranquility, we wish to bring our best care for you and your loved ones in need during a time of difficulty. We took this word as a driving force to envision the type of care that we provide for our patients and families that are involved in the care.

Hospice is a special care that provides comfort care when aggressive treatment is no longer an option. Our interdisciplinary team consists of a physician nurse, social worker, chaplain, hospice aide, volunteer, and pharmacist. Our goals are to keep you as comfortable as possible, and to address any physical, psychosocial, emotional, spiritual, nursing, and bereavement care you may require, as well as the needs of your loved ones.

Mission

Maluhia Hospice Care, PLLC is committed to treat all patients and families with peace, tranquility, and serenity during the end of life with highest level of quality care, respect, compassion, and support.

Philosophy:

We recognize dying as a natural, inevitable process. When the curative therapy is no longer effective, we seek neither to hasten nor prolong the dying process. Our team believes that palliative care and sensitivity to an individual‘s needs will allow dying patients to experience full and rewarding lives until death. While supporting family and caregivers during the patient’s illness and assist them after the patient’s death through the bereavement program. Affirms life and regards dying as a normal process. Neither hastens nor postpones death. Provide relief from pain and other physical distressing symptoms.
